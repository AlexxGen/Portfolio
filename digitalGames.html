Digital Games

Hidden Pines

In this 2D platformer, the player plays Evelyn, a girl who has struggled with misfortune all her life. The game starts with her moving to a new town that is not as peaceful as the residents let on. I made most art assets and programmed the player movements and character animations. - May 2024 -

Tickler Tycoon

This Tycoon game was submitted to the Global Game Jam 2024 where the theme was "Make Me Laugh". In this game, the main character is a kindergartener who spends his recess running around tickling his classmates. I drew all of the art and programmed the player movements and character animations. - April 2024 -

Raindrop Race

In this physics game, the player controls a raindrop as it races down the car window against other other raindrops. The raindrop must get to the bottom of the window first all while avoiding the big water spots that would wash it away. This was a solo project where I programmed the whole game. - March 2024 -
