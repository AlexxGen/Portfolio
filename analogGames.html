Analog Games

Avarisia

An RPG rouge-like where four players assume the roles as adventures, and one player takes the role as the dungeon. The adventurers must battle the monsters within the dungeon, while remaining head strong as to not fall victim to their deepest desires. I worked on the art for all of the monsters and characters as well as designed the various player sheets. - October 2023 -
