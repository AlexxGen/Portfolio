Spotlight Project

Hidden Pines

In this 2D platformer, the player plays Evelyn, a girl who has struggled with misfortune all her life. The game starts with her moving to a new town that is not as peaceful as the residents let on. I made most art assets and programmed the player movements and character animations. - May 2024 -

About Me

I am currently a Sophomore Computer Science and Game Development Major at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in Troy, New York. Whether I be working in a team or solo, I have honed my skills in Unity, particularly Unity 2D, and embraced creative expression through art asset creation on Autodesk Sketchbook over the past year. One of my proudest moments was presenting Avarisia alongside my team at the RPI Game Dev club's Games Showcase Beyond Game development, in the Fall of 2023, I did a virtual externship with Matchmaker, a dating website for singles in Utah. Through this experience, I delved into the fundamentals of web development and provided a different perspective for inclusivity for the website.
